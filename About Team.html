About Team



We are Team Anubis


We are students at the Egyptian-Korean Technological College for Energy and Industry in Beni Suef. Our college has been significantly enhanced through support from the Korean delegation, which has provided us with advanced resources and capabilities. As a team of eight enthusiastic students, we are passionate about leveraging modern technology and artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems. Our project idea emerged from a common issue observed among our peers: many students struggle to find someone to answer their questions or feel too embarrassed to ask for help.
